Fall off the bone ribs!

Description

    Rib ingredients

  • 2 to 2 1/2 pounds baby back pork ribs
  • Salt and black pepper
    • Sweet & Spicy BBQ Sauce ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup finely diced onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon hot chili sauce (Sriracha can be used as an alternative!)
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • salt & ground pepper, for taste testing
Recipe Directions
Step 1 -

You will begin with removing the baby back ribs membrane. (Very important for the serving portion , allowing rib bones to come out clean)

 Step 2 -

Season each side equally , with a genorous amount allowing you to add any favorite spice rub if you'd like.

 Step 3 -

Once the ribs are genorously covered with rub and/or seasoned well, you will then wrap the ribs fully with aluminum foil. Make sure to have all gaps or openings covered keeping the ribs liquids , etc.. inside.

 Step 4 -

Bake ribs at a low temperature (275Â°F) for 2 1/2 to 4 hours or until the ribs are tender.

 Step 5 -

Once tender slather the baked ribs with barbecue sauce. Once coated broil or grill ribs for a couple minutes until the barbecue sauce is caramalized.
